Quick Sleep Tips

Optimal Sleep Duration

Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night. Quality matters more than quantity - aim for complete sleep cycles.

Room Temperature

Keep your bedroom between 60-67°F (15-19°C). A cooler environment promotes deeper, more restful sleep.

Screen Time

Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime. Blue light can disrupt your natural circadian rhythm and melatonin production.

Caffeine Timing

Stop consuming caffeine 6 hours before bedtime. Caffeine can stay in your system longer than you think.

Exercise Timing

Regular exercise improves sleep quality, but avoid vigorous workouts 3 hours before bedtime.

Relaxation Routine

Develop a consistent bedtime routine. Try reading, meditation, or gentle stretching to signal sleep time.
